Hello!

I am Mahdi Sadjadi, a physics PhD candidate at ASU. My research combines experimental results, computer simulations and mathematical theorems to gain a better understanding of glass structure. I study under Mike Thorpe.

Apart from physics, I interact with computers and make them do part of my work! Using mostly python and its great arsenal of tools, I visualize and analyze datasets, especially data with social aspects. I’m also an instructor at Software Carpentry.
